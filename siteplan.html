Website Project Plan - Exploring Malawi's Cultural Heritage

Project Subject

Subject: Malawi Cultural Heritage

Description of Planned Content

  • Traditional music and dances
  • Art and cuisine
  • Historical landmarks
  • Cultural festivals and events

Reason for Selection

Malawi’s cultural heritage is rich and diverse. Creating a platform to showcase it provides an opportunity to connect global audiences to Malawi’s vibrant cultural identity while preserving its history.

Site Name

Example: Malawi Cultural Heritage

This name encapsulates the purpose of showcasing Malawi's cultural richness, making it memorable and reflective of the site’s content.

Optional Domain Availability: malawi-heritage.com

Site Purpose

The site provides information about Malawi's traditional music, dances, art, cuisine, and historical landmarks. It also highlights cultural festivals and events, connecting global audiences with Malawi's unique cultural identity.

Scenarios

  • What are the most iconic traditional dances in Malawi?
  • Where can I find information about Malawi's cultural festivals?
