Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Malawi University Guide

This site will serve as an online guide to universities in Malawi. The name is short, descriptive, and speaks directly to students and learners seeking higher education opportunities in the country.

Site Purpose

This website provides a user-friendly directory and comparison tool for universities in Malawi. It will include university profiles, contact information, available programs, admission requirements, rankings, and a map view of campuses. Its goal is to help students and parents make informed decisions about higher education in Malawi.

Scenarios

  • What public universities in Malawi offer degrees in engineering or agriculture?
  • Where is MUBAS located and how can I apply?
  • Which private universities have distance learning options in Malawi?

Color Scheme

  • #0057b8 (Deep Blue): For headers, buttons, and navigation elements to establish professionalism and trust.
  • #f2f2f2 (Light Gray): Background color for body to maintain readability and simplicity.
  • #ffffff (White): Content boxes and sections for contrast.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for all headings for a clean and modern look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body content to ensure readability on all devices.
